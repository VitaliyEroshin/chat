 
List of available commands:
    /addfriend {id}     add user to your friendlist by id.
    /myid               show your account id.
    /chat               view your current chat id.
    /makechat           make a new chat.
    /invite {id}        invite user to the chat you are in.
    /switchchat {id}    switch your chat by chatid.
    /friends            view your friendlist.
    /chats              view available chats.

    /about              view app description.

    /quit               leave the app.
